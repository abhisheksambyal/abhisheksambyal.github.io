I am a Ph.D. Student in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Ropar, India, where I work on representation learning for medical image analysis.

My research focuses on designing and developing Machine Learning and Computer Vision based solutions to healthcare related challenges. Specifically, I am exploring avenues to efficiently address the High Dimensionality, Low Sample Size (HDLSS) characteristics of medical images within deep learning frameworks.
